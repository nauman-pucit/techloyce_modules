ODOO 10.0 Salesforce Connector

Sync Salesforce with odoo using Salesforce Connector

Overview

ODOO is a fully integrated suite of business modules that encompass the traditional ERP functionality. Odoo integration with Salesforce gives us power to sync customer, account, sale orders etc.

Key Features And Functions

  • Import Customers from Salesforce to Odoo
  • Import Sale Orders from Salesforce to Odoo
  • Export Product to Salesforce from Odoo

Salesforce Credentials

  • Individual Salesforce account for odoo user.
  • Salesforce credentials verification using test credentials.

Import/Export Options

  • This is the wizard from which the customers, products and sale orders can be imported/exported

Sale Orders

  • Imported sale orders from Salesforce to Odoo
  • Sale orders with no customer will not be imported to Odoo, as customer is required field in Odoo sale order.

Customers

  • Imported customers from Salesforce to Odoo

Products

  • Exported products to Salesforce from Odoo

Requirements

  • Install python package simple-salesforce through this command: pip install simple_salesforce
  • Should have an Salesforce account having customers, accounts and sale orders.
